WHC PROGRAM DESCRIPTION (Plan 7000)

A. GENERAL SCOPE &amp; COVERAGE
(1.) Annual Maximum Benefit
(2.) Private Room and Board
(3.) Professional Fee
(4.) Drugs and Medicines
(5.) Other Hospital Services
(6.) Out-Patient Consultation and/or Referral to Medical Specialist
(7.) Compulsory Annual Physical Examination
(8.) Maternity Services
(9.) Out-Patient Dental Care Services
(10.) Emergency Care Services in Non-Accredited Hospitals or Physicians
(11.) Mortuary Assistance
B. RATE, SCHEDULE AND COST OF BENEFITS
I. RATE

Term Rate

Annual Rate/Member ₱ 7,000.00
II. BENEFITS
a. Annual Maximum Benefit (AMB) : Seventy Thousand Pesos (₱70,000.00)
b. Basic Hospital Benefits
1. Room and Board : Covered up to Private Room but

not to exceed AMB

2. Professional Fee : Covered but not to exceed AMB
3. Drugs and Medicines : Covered but not to exceed AMB
4. Other Hospital Services : Covered but not to exceed AMB
c. Preventive Healthcare Benefits
1. Annual Physical Examination : Available per Program Description
2. Out-Patient Consultation : Covered per Program Description
3. Referral to Medical Specialist : Covered per Program Description
d. Other Benefits and Assistance
1. Maternity Services : Available per Program Description
2. Out-Patient Dental Care : Covered per Program Description
3. Emergency Care in
Non-accredited Hospital : Covered per Program Description
4. Mortuary Assistance : Covered per Program Description

C. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

SERVICES COST &amp; DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES
1.

Annual Maximum
Benefit (AMB)

Total or accumulated cost of availment by a member for a
period of one (1) year in the amount of SEVENTY
THOUSAND PESOS (₱70,000.00), to include the cost for
the basic hospital benefits such as Room and Board,
Professional Fee, Drugs and Medicines, Other Hospital
Services; Preventive Healthcare Benefits; and Other
Benefits and Assistance.

2.
Room &amp; Board

Maximum of ₱1,000.00 per day of confinement in a Private
Room on top of PhilHealth coverage up to AMB limit. The
coverage includes Emergency Room Fee while awaiting for
room confinement.

3.
Professional Fee Maximum of ₱1,000.00 per day of confinement on top of

PhilHealth coverage up to AMB limit.

4. Drugs and
Medicines

Maximum coverage of ₱12,000.00 for every single period of
confinement up to AMB limit.

5.
Other Hospital
Services

Maximum coverage of ₱8,000.00 for every single period of
confinement up to AMB limit. The services include in-patient
laboratory procedures, tests, and examinations; and such
other hospital charges and fees which are medically
necessary.

6.
Out-Patient
Consultation
and/or referral to
medical
specialists

Unlimited out-of-hospital consultation services to members
which includes medical check-up, laboratory, minor surgery,
treatment of minor illness or injury, vaccination but excluding
the prescribed vaccines, referral to accredited medical
specialists, health education or counseling on diet, exercise,
and family planning.

7.
Annual Physical
Examination/
Laboratory

Blood chemistry for Fasting Blood Sugar, Creatinine, SGPT,
and Uric Acid; Electrocardiogram (ECG), Chest X-Ray, and
taking of complete medical history. These clinical
procedures are exclusive, or on top, of the AMB of a
member.

8.
Maternity
Services

Financial assistance of ₱10,000.00 for Caesarean Section
delivery; ₱5,000.00 for miscarriage case; five (5) pre-natal
examinations, and one (1) post-natal examination for any
type of delivery.

9.
Out-Patient
Dental Care
Services

Includes four (4) dental extractions per year, one (1) simple
annual prophylaxis, two (2) temporary fillings, and treatment
of oral pain, lesion and burn.

10. Emergency Care
Services in Non-
Accredited
Hospitals and/or

Reimbursement or immediate cash settlement to health
facility of eighty percent (80%) of the total hospital bills, to
include room and board, professional fee, drugs and

by Non-
Accredited
Physicians

medicines, and other hospital services, subject to AMB limit
and Program Description.

11.
Mortuary
Assistance

Death benefit of ₱5,000.00 in case death is due to natural
cause or ₱10,000.00 if death is due to accident. This benefit
is exclusive, or on top, of the AMB of a member.
