PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:
Services Cost &amp; Description of Services
1.
Annual Maximum
Benefit (AMB)
limit

Accumulated cost of availment by a member for a period of one
(1) year in the amount of SIXTY THOUSAND PESOS
(P60,000.00), to include the cost for the basic hospital benefits
such as Room and Board, Professional Fee, Drugs and
Medicines, Other Hospital Services; Preventive Healthcare
Benefits; and Other Benefits and Assistance.

2.
Room &amp; Board Maximum of P600.00 per day of confinement on top of PhilHealth

coverage up to AMB limit.

3.
Professional Fee Maximum of P600.00 per day of confinement on top of PhilHealth

coverage up to AMB limit.

4. Drugs and
Medicines

Maximum coverage of P6,000.00 for every single period of
confinement up to AMB limit.

5. Other Hospital
Services

Maximum coverage of P4,000.00 for every single period of
confinement up to AMB limit.

6. Out-Patient
Consultation
and/or referral to
medical
specialists

Unlimited out-of-hospital consultation services to members which
includes medical check-up, laboratory, minor surgery, treatment of
minor illness or injury, vaccination but excluding the prescribed
vaccines, referral to accredited medical specialists, health
education or counseling on diet, exercise, and family planning.

7.
Physical
Examination/
Laboratory

Physical Examination, Chest X-Ray, Complete Blood Count,
Urinalysis, Fecalysis, Electrocardiogram, Uric Acid, Fasting Blood
Sugar, Pap Smear for female members 35 years old and above,
and taking of complete medical history.

8.
Maternity
Services

Financial assistance of P10,000.00 for caesarian section delivery;
P5,000.00 for miscarriage case; five (5) pre-natal examinations,
and one (1) post-natal examination for any type of delivery.

9. Out-Patient
Dental Care
Services

Includes four (4) dental extractions per year, one (1) simple annual
prophylaxis, two (2) temporary fillings, and treatment of oral pain,
lesion and burn.

10. Emergency Care
Services in Non-
Accredited

Reimbursement or immediate cash settlement to health facility of
eighty percent (80%) of the total hospital bills, to include room and
board, professional fee, drugs and medicines, and other hospital

Hospitals and/or
by Non-
Accredited
Physicians

services, subject to AMB limit and Program Description.

11. Mortuary
Assistance

Death benefit of ₱5,000.00 in case death is due to natural cause
or ₱10,000.00 if death is due to accident.
